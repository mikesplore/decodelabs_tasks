[Para 1] Manchester City is a professional football club based in Manchester, England, competing in the Premier League. The club was founded in 1880 under the name St. Mark’s and later became Manchester City in 1894.

[Para 2] It plays its home matches at the Etihad Stadium, which has a capacity of over 50,000 spectators. Under recent management, the team has become known for its possession-based playing style and tactical dominance.

[Para 3] Manchester City has won multiple domestic league titles and continues to be one of the strongest teams in European football.
